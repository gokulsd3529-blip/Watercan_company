About Namakkal Water Can

Our Story

Started by local entrepreneurs, Namakkal Water Can grew from a neighbourhood service into the town's most trusted supplier by focusing on quality and friendly delivery. We combine modern purification technology with locally-sourced water and a circular can program.

Our Process

  1. Source testing & treatment
  2. Multi-stage filtration (sediment, RO/UF, activated carbon)
  3. UV sterilisation and mineral rebalancing
  4. Hygienic filling & sealed packaging
